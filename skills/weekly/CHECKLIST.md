# Weekly review checklist

The canonical GTD three-part review, in pure GTD terms — no tool specifics
(those live in MLO.md). Edit freely: reorder, delete steps, add your own.

## 1. Get clear

- **Empty the inbox.** If the inbox has items, run the full clarify & organize
  flow (the `inbox` skill) before anything else — reviewing on top of an
  unprocessed inbox reviews stale data.
- **Mini mindsweep.** Ask: "Anything on your mind that isn't in the system
  yet?" Capture answers straight into the inbox and process them with the rest.

## 2. Get current

- **Overdue and due soon.** For each overdue item: still relevant? Reschedule,
  do, or drop. Preview everything due in the coming week.
- **Projects sweep.** For every active project: is the outcome still wanted?
  Does it satisfy the next-action policy? A project with nothing actionable
  left is either done or blocked — figure out which.
- **Next actions sweep.** Are actions still phrased as concrete physical
  actions? Anything stale past the threshold — renegotiate or delete.
- **Waiting-for.** Has each wait resolved? Does anything need a follow-up
  nudge?
- **Completed last week.** A quick wins recap — it often surfaces follow-ups
  worth capturing.

## 3. Get creative

- **Someday/Maybe.** Anything to activate into a live project? Anything dead
  to delete?
- **New ideas.** Ask: "Given everything you just saw — any new projects or
  ideas to capture?" Straight to the inbox, no organizing now.
