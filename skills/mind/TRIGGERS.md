# Mindsweep trigger lists

Condensed from the classic GTD incompletion triggers. Offer a category at a
time; the prompts are conversation starters, not a questionnaire to march
through. Edit to fit your life — delete what never applies, add what does.

## Professional

- **Projects started, not finished** — commitments made to others; things you
  said you'd get back to someone about.
- **Communications** — emails, calls, messages you owe or are waiting on;
  things to discuss with specific people next time you talk.
- **Writing & reading** — reports, docs, reviews to write; things saved "to
  read later".
- **Meetings & decisions** — meetings to schedule or prepare; decisions you've
  been putting off.
- **Career & development** — skills to learn, things to research, professional
  relationships to maintain.
- **Systems & tools** — things that are broken, slow, or annoying in your
  setup; backups, upgrades, subscriptions.

## Personal

- **Family & people** — birthdays, occasions, promises to family and friends,
  people you've been meaning to contact.
- **Home** — repairs, improvements, things to buy, things to return or get rid
  of; car and other possessions needing attention.
- **Health** — appointments to book, checkups due, habits you keep meaning to
  start.
- **Finance & admin** — bills, taxes, renewals, insurance, documents to file
  or renew.
- **Errands** — things to pick up, drop off, or buy next time you're out.
- **Leisure & someday** — trips you talk about taking, hobbies to start or
  restart, things you'd do "if you had time".

## Closing sweep

- "What else has your attention right now, big or trivial?"
- "Anything you're low-grade worried about forgetting?"
